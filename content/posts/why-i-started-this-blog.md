After years of writing in various places — Twitter threads, newsletters, notes apps — I decided to build my own simple blog.

## Why a personal site?

There's something valuable about having a home on the internet that you fully control. Social platforms come and go. Algorithms change. But a simple blog is yours.

## Why not WordPress?

I used WordPress for years, but it was compromised. The attack surface is just too large — plugins, themes, database, PHP. This new site is intentionally minimal:

- No database to hack
- No plugins to exploit
- No user accounts to compromise

Just markdown files rendered into clean pages.

## What I'll write about

Expect a mix of:

- **Health** — things I've learned about optimizing energy, sleep, and performance
- **Startups** — patterns from evaluating and building companies
- **Ideas** — things I find interesting that don't fit neatly into a category

Thanks for reading.
